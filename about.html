About Us

Savanna Junior Foundation: Empowering the Future of Africa's Youth

The **Savanna Junior Foundation** is dedicated to creating a future where every African child and adolescent can thrive. We work tirelessly to uplift endangered children and young people across Africa, offering them access to essential resources in education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. Our initiatives are both innovative and community-driven, ensuring that each program is tailored to the unique needs of the communities we serve.

Our mission is rooted in compassion, empowerment, and a commitment to making a sustainable difference. We partner with local communities, donors, and volunteers to build a network of support that allows us to reach children in need, providing them with a safe and nurturing environment where they can grow and flourish. Through our evidence-based programs, we aim to not only meet the immediate needs of our beneficiaries but also to provide long-term solutions that help shape a brighter, more resilient future for Africa.

Through our programs, we strive to foster a sense of hope, compassion, and resilience in young lives. Our vision is clear: to see an Africa where every child has the chance to succeed, to live with dignity, and to contribute to their community’s well-being. We are committed to nurturing talent, encouraging ambition, and supporting the dreams of Africa’s future generations.

Meet Our Team

Dedicated to Empowering Africa’s Youth

Our team is committed to driving impactful change and empowering African children and adolescents through innovative, community-driven initiatives. Meet the passionate individuals leading the way.

Emmanuel Udofia
Founder/Executive Director

JEmmanuel Udofia is a skilled qualitative researcher and data analyst who specializes in the use of Nvivo, Atlas.ti and other analytic tools to support data-driven decisions. He has background in broadcast journalism, nonprofit program management and a strong community engagement, especially with children and adolescents. He has led the team at Savanna Junior Care Foundation in the design and execution of community-based projects focused on education, health and youth empowerment, addressing critical issues such as drug abuse prevention and life skills development. Emmanuel leverages his skills in research, collaboration and community-engagement to drive the foundation's mission to create opportunities for young lives to and create sustainable change in underserved communities.

Oluwaseun Newton
Trustee/Advisor, Healthcare Strategy & Policy

Oluwaseun Newton is a dedicated healthcare specialist with experience in medicine and public administration. Her global experience spans customer support, consulting and management roles in healthcare where she has developed a deep understanding of patient care and policy development. Passionate about advancing digital health and advancing digital healthcare policies, Oluwaseun is committed to meaningful change in African communities. In her advisory role at Savanna Junior Care Foundation, she lends her expertise in health education and digital solutions in helping to shape the foundation's healthcare strategy that focuses on sustainable health education and innovation health solutions for African communities. With her expertise in healthcare policy, digital innovation and public administration, she advises on strategic directions for healthcare initiatives and digital solutions.

Chinyere Adeyemo
Trustee/Advisor, Child Protection, Family Education and Regulatory Engagement

Chinyere Adeyemo is a passionate advocate for child protection, committed to empowering families with knowledge and tools for safe and informed parenting. Known for her impactful seminars on child protection, parenting, and sex education, she brings compassion and practical wisdom to each session, aiming to help parents raise well-rounded children with confidence. In her role at Savanna Junior Care Foundation as the Advisor for Child Protection, Family Education, and Regulatory Engagement, Chinyere guides the foundation’s initiatives in child safety, family education, and compliance with regulatory standards. Her insights from the child protection network and her knowledge of government regulations are instrumental in shaping the foundation’s approach to safeguarding children and supporting families in an ever-evolving society.

Segun Alonge Jr.
Director, Leadership, Research and Program Innovation

Segun Alonge Jr. is an inspiring educator, leadership consultant, and founder of PARASHIFT NG, a nonprofit dedicated to mentoring youth toward intentional living and leadership development. With a passion for addressing social, mental, and spiritual challenges, Segun works to unite communities and ignite change. His extensive experience with social impact organizations enriches his role at Savanna Junior Care Foundation, where he helps bridge the gap between potential and opportunity for African youth. When he’s not consulting, he enjoys nature, crafting parables, and indulging in crunchy fruits.

Oluwakemi Olanrewaju
Volunteer Coordinator

Oluwakemi is a dedicated professional with a background in Materials and Metallurgical Engineering and hands-on experience in research and administration, with career roles in research and development. At Savanna Junior Care Foundation, Oluwakemi leads with a focus on meticulous planning and effective team coordination. She manages volunteer recruitment, training and engagement, ensuring that all team members are aligned with the foundation’s mission. Her experience in research coordination and team leadership empowers her to foster a collaborative environment, making a positive impact on the lives of children and families the foundation serves.
